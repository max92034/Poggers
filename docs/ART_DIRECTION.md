# Art Direction — Soft Retro-Future Urbanism

> Distilled from `src/art/2026-07-04-pog-world-concept-design.md` + the five
> concept boards (2026-07-04). This is the implementable token sheet; the
> brief is the source of truth for intent.

## One-line thesis

**2000s anime future technology trapped inside 1000-year-old real estate.**
A functional, attractive civilization that has normalized settling all
conflict by POG. Played completely straight — never parody, never ruins,
never generic neon cyberpunk.

## Tone stack (every scene holds all three)

1. **Surface** — calm, domestic, attractive, quietly uncanny
2. **Underside** — humid, cramped, economically stressed
3. **Core corruption** — stylish, ritualized, transactional

## Material rules

- **Old bones, new skin:** every structure = weathered concrete/tile/steel
  shell + translucent polymer / glowing acrylic retrofit. Never all-new,
  never all-ruin.
- Consumer tech is rounded, optimistic, desirable — no military hardware.
- Undercity uses the SAME structural bones in worse light. It must read as
  the same city, one floor down.

## The two arenas (in-game venues)

### Official Arena — "state venue"

- Civic broadcast bowl: clean tiers, even cool lighting, ceremonial calm
- Light `concrete-gray`/`dusty-cream` floor, `vending-cyan` ring markings,
  `oxidized-teal` trim, soft sky ambient
- Feel: televised fairness. "Make fairness legible to the whole city."
- Rules: weigh-in enforced — rivals play clean chips; mods are inspected

### Underground Arena — "hidden venue"

- Salvage tunnel den: `sodium-orange` tube lamps as key light,
  `dirty-magenta` fill, `oil-black` shadows, `wet-gray` floor
- Ring painted rough, `signal-red`/orange
- Feel: rough, socially exposed, but the POG rule stays sacred
- Rules: anything goes — rivals bring burned/painted (later: washer) chips

## Chips

- Colorful round caps (see Underground Vignette) — collectible art on top
  faces later; mods must stay physically visible (crust, scorch, dome)
- The workbench IS an undercity repair stall (REPAIR kiosk) — sodium
  light, chips spread on a table

## Characters

- Female-majority world (1:10); style tribes: Goth, Idol, Sport, Urban
  Grace — rivals draw from these; existing roster (Aoi/Lumina/Kuroe) fits
- Protagonist: 2008 cryo-survivor; his mods/skills are lost technology

## Avoid list (from the brief, binding)

- generic neon cyberpunk clichés
- post-apocalyptic ruin aesthetics
- parody or overt comedy treatment
- ultra-clean utopian sci-fi with no history
- militarized hardware
- an underworld that reads as a different city
